# Lesson 04: Daily Routines

## 本课目标

学完本课后，你可以：

- 描述早上、工作/学习、晚上三个日常片段。
- 使用 `I usually...`, `I often...`, `I sometimes...` 组织习惯。
- 听日常描述时，按时间顺序拆句。

## 1. 日常动词

| 英语 | 中文 |
| --- | --- |
| wake up | 醒来 |
| get up | 起床 |
| have breakfast | 吃早餐 |
| go to work | 去上班 |
| study English | 学英语 |
| have dinner | 吃晚餐 |
| go home | 回家 |
| go to bed | 睡觉 |

## 2. 频率词

| 英语 | 中文 | 例句 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| always | 总是 | I always drink water. |
| usually | 通常 | I usually get up at seven. |
| often | 经常 | I often study English. |
| sometimes | 有时候 | I sometimes drink coffee. |
| never | 从不 | I never smoke. |

## 3. 场景：怎么描述自己一天

按时间线组织：

1. **早上**：In the morning, I ...
2. **白天**：In the afternoon, I ...
3. **晚上**：In the evening, I ...
4. **补充频率**：I usually / often / sometimes ...

### 模板

```text
In the morning, I get up at seven.
I have breakfast and go to work.
In the evening, I study English.
I usually go to bed at eleven.
```

如果不会说完整时间，也可以先说：

```text
I study English every day.
```

## 4. 听力：按时间顺序拆句

日常描述常按时间推进。听的时候先画三栏：

```text
Morning: ________
Afternoon: ________
Evening: ________
```

例句：

```text
I get up at seven, / go to work at nine, / and study English at night.
```

拆成三块：

```text
get up / 7
work / 9
study English / night
```

### 不必听清的小词

`I`, `at`, `and`, `to` 这些小词常常弱读。初学阶段先听动词和时间：`get up`, `seven`, `work`, `night`。

## 5. 小对话

```text
A: What do you do every day?
B: I get up at seven.
A: Do you study English every day?
B: Yes. I usually study English at night.
A: That’s great.
```

### 意群拆句

```text
What do you do / every day?
I get up / at seven.
I usually study English / at night.
```

## 6. 练习

### 练习 1：三句说一天

```text
In the morning, I ________.
In the afternoon, I ________.
In the evening, I ________.
```

### 练习 2：频率替换

```text
I always ________.
I usually ________.
I sometimes ________.
```

### 练习 3：听力抓动词

听别人说日常时，只写动词短语：`get up`, `go to work`, `study English`。先不要纠结冠词和介词。

## 7. 本课检查

- 能说 8 个日常动作。
- 能用时间线说 4 句自己的日常。
- 听日常描述时能按 morning / afternoon / evening 拆句。
