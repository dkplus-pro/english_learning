# Lesson 05: Food and Drinks

## 本课目标

学完本课后，你可以：

- 在咖啡店或简单餐厅用英语点餐。
- 使用 `I’d like...`, `Can I have...?` 礼貌表达需求。
- 听服务员问题时，抓“品类、数量、冷热、堂食/带走”。

## 2. 点餐日常句

| 英语 | 中文 |
| --- | --- |
| I’d like a coffee. | 我想要一杯咖啡。 |
| Can I have a tea? | 我可以要一杯茶吗？ |
| One coffee, please. | 一杯咖啡，谢谢。 |
| For here or to go? | 堂食还是带走？ |
| To go, please. | 带走，谢谢。 |
| That’s all. | 就这些。 |

## 3. 场景：点餐怎么组织语言

顺序：

1. **打招呼**：Hi. / Hello.
2. **说需求**：I’d like ...
3. **说数量/规格**：one / two / small / large
4. **回答附加问题**：for here / to go, hot / iced
5. **结束**：That’s all. Thank you.

### 模板

```text
Hi. I’d like a coffee, please.
A small coffee.
To go, please.
That’s all. Thank you.
```

更简单：

```text
One coffee, please.
To go, please.
```

## 4. 听力：菜单场景听什么

服务员常问这些问题：

```text
What would you like?
Anything else?
For here or to go?
Small or large?
Hot or iced?
```

听的时候抓最后的选择词：

- `here / to go`
- `small / large`
- `hot / iced`
- `else` 表示“还要别的吗”

### 不是所有都要听清

如果你听到 `small or large`，只需要回答大小：

```text
Small, please.
```

没听清整句也可以根据场景回答。

## 5. 小对话

```text
A: Hi. What would you like?
B: I’d like a coffee, please.
A: Small or large?
B: Small, please.
A: For here or to go?
B: To go, please.
A: Anything else?
B: That’s all. Thank you.
```

### 意群拆句

```text
What would you like?
I’d like / a coffee, please.
Small / or large?
For here / or to go?
That’s all. / Thank you.
```

## 6. 练习

### 练习 1：三种点法

用三种方式点同一样东西：

```text
I’d like ________.
Can I have ________?
One ________, please.
```

### 练习 2：快速回答

- `Small or large?` → `Small, please.`
- `For here or to go?` → `To go, please.`
- `Anything else?` → `That’s all.`

### 练习 3：听力标签

听菜单对话时，只做标签：

```text
Item: ________
Size: ________
Here/To go: ________
```

## 7. 本课检查

- 能用至少 2 种方式点一杯饮料。
- 能回答堂食/带走、大小、还要不要。
- 听菜单场景时能抓选择词，不要求听完整句。
