# Lesson 06: Directions and Transport

## 本课目标

学完本课后，你可以：

- 问某个地点在哪里，以及如何到达。
- 听懂基础方向词：left, right, straight, near, next to。
- 在迷路时用短句求助，不需要说复杂路线。

## 1. 地点和交通词

| 英语 | 中文 |
| --- | --- |
| station | 车站 |
| airport | 机场 |

## 2. 方向词

| 英语 | 中文 | 例句 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| left | 左 | Turn left. |
| right | 右 | Turn right. |
| straight | 直走 | Go straight. |
| near | 附近 | It’s near the station. |
| next to | 在旁边 | It’s next to the cafe. |
| across from | 在对面 | It’s across from the hotel. |

## 3. 场景：问路怎么组织语言

顺序：

1. **礼貌开头**：Excuse me.
2. **说需求**：Where is ...? / How can I get to ...?
3. **确认听懂**：Left? Right? / Go straight?
4. **感谢**：Thank you.

### 模板

```text
Excuse me.
Where is the subway station?
Go straight and turn left?
Thank you.
```

如果听不懂路线，直接简化：

```text
Sorry, can you show me on the map?
```

## 4. 听力：路线说明怎么拆

路线通常是动作序列：

```text
Go straight / for two blocks, / turn right, / and it’s next to the bank.
```

先只抓动作：

```text
go straight → turn right → next to bank
```

### 关键提醒

- 听方向时，`left` 和 `right` 很关键，没听清必须确认。
- `for two blocks` 这类距离信息可以后补。
- 不需要听清每个介词；先听动作和地标。

## 5. 小对话

```text
A: Excuse me. Where is the hotel?
B: Go straight and turn right.
A: Turn right?
B: Yes. It’s next to the cafe.
A: Thank you.
```

### 意群拆句

```text
Excuse me. / Where is the hotel?
Go straight / and turn right.
It’s next to / the cafe.
```

## 6. 练习

### 练习 1：问三个地点

```text
Excuse me. Where is the restroom?
Excuse me. Where is the station?
Excuse me. Where is the hotel?
```

### 练习 2：听动作链

听路线时只写箭头：

```text
straight → left → station
right → next to cafe
```

### 练习 3：确认句

如果没听清，使用：

```text
Sorry, left or right?
Could you say that again?
Can you show me on the map?
```

## 7. 本课检查

- 能说 `Excuse me. Where is...?`
- 能听懂 left/right/straight/next to。
- 知道路线听力先抓动作链和地标。
