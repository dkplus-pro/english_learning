# Lesson 07: Shopping and Prices

## 本课目标

学完本课后，你可以：

- 询问价格、颜色、尺寸，并完成简单购物。
- 用 `How much is it?`, `Do you have...?` 组织购物需求。
- 听价格时抓货币、整数和小数，不被快速报价打乱。

## 3. 场景：购物怎么组织语言

顺序：

1. **看中物品**：I like this one.
2. **问价格**：How much is it?
3. **问选项**：Do you have it in ...? / Do you have a ... size?
4. **决定**：I’ll take it. / I’ll think about it.
5. **付款**：Can I pay by card?

### 模板

```text
I like this one.
How much is it?
Do you have it in black?
I’ll take it.
Can I pay by card?
```

## 4. 听力：价格怎么听

价格通常重读数字：

```text
It’s fifteen dollars.
It’s twenty-nine ninety-nine.
```

听价格时先写数字，不要马上翻译：

```text
15 dollars
29.99
```

### 没听清怎么办

```text
Sorry, how much?
Could you repeat the price?
```

价格、方向、时间属于“必须确认”的信息。没听清不要猜。

## 5. 小对话

```text
A: Hi. How much is this shirt?
B: It’s twenty dollars.
A: Do you have it in black?
B: Yes, we do.
A: Great. I’ll take it. Can I pay by card?
B: Sure.
```

### 意群拆句

```text
How much / is this shirt?
It’s twenty dollars.
Do you have it / in black?
I’ll take it.
Can I pay / by card?
```

## 6. 练习

### 练习 1：替换颜色和尺寸

```text
Do you have it in ________?
Do you have a ________ size?
```

颜色：black, white, blue, red, green

尺寸：small, medium, large

### 练习 2：价格听写只写数字

听到价格时只写：

```text
$12
$25
$39.99
```

### 练习 3：两种结尾

```text
I’ll take it.
I’ll think about it. Thank you.
```

## 7. 本课检查

- 能问价格、颜色、尺寸和付款方式。
- 听价格时知道先抓数字，没听清必须确认。
- 能用 4–5 句完成一次购物对话。
