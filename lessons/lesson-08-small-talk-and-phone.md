# Lesson 08: Small Talk and Phone Calls

## 本课目标

学完本课后，你可以：

- 进行简单寒暄：天气、近况、周末。
- 接电话或语音通话时完成开场、说明目的、确认信息。
- 听电话英语时，学会请求重复和拼写确认。

## 1. 寒暄话题

| 话题 | 句子 | 中文 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 天气 | Nice weather today. | 今天天气不错。 |
| 近况 | How’s your day? | 今天过得怎么样？ |
| 周末 | How was your weekend? | 周末怎么样？ |
| 工作 | Busy day? | 今天忙吗？ |
| 学习 | I’m practicing English. | 我在练英语。 |

## 2. 电话常用句

| 英语 | 中文 |
| --- | --- |
| Hello, this is Li Ming. | 你好，我是李明。 |
| May I speak to Anna? | 我可以找 Anna 吗？ |
| Who’s calling, please? | 请问哪位？ |
| Can you hear me? | 你能听到我吗？ |
| Could you repeat that? | 你能重复一下吗？ |
| Let me confirm. | 我确认一下。 |

## 3. 场景：电话怎么组织语言

顺序：

1. **自报姓名**：Hello, this is ...
2. **说明找谁/目的**：May I speak to ...? / I’m calling about ...
3. **确认信息**：Let me confirm ...
4. **结束**：Thank you. Talk to you later.

### 模板

```text
Hello, this is Li Ming.
May I speak to Anna?
I’m calling about our meeting.
Let me confirm: tomorrow at three.
Thank you.
```

## 4. 听力：电话为什么更难

电话里缺少表情和口型，声音也可能不清楚。所以策略是：

- 先听身份：Who is calling?
- 再听目的：Why are they calling?
- 最后确认细节：time, place, phone number, name spelling

### 必备确认句

```text
Sorry, I didn’t catch that.
Could you say that again?
Could you spell your name?
Let me repeat: tomorrow at three, right?
```

电话场景中，数字、姓名、时间不必靠猜，应该主动确认。

## 5. 小对话

```text
A: Hello, this is Anna.
B: Hi Anna, this is Li Ming.
A: Hi Li Ming. Can you hear me?
B: Yes. I’m calling about our meeting.
A: Tomorrow at three?
B: Yes. Tomorrow at three.
```

### 意群拆句

```text
Hello, / this is Anna.
Can you hear me?
I’m calling about / our meeting.
Tomorrow / at three?
```

## 6. 练习

### 练习 1：寒暄三句

```text
Nice weather today.
How’s your day?
I’m practicing English.
```

### 练习 2：电话确认

用下面句子确认你听到的信息：

```text
Let me confirm: ________, right?
```

### 练习 3：没听清也能继续

连续练 5 遍：

```text
Sorry, I didn’t catch that.
Could you say that again?
```

## 7. 本课检查

- 能完成电话开场：`Hello, this is...`
- 能用至少 3 句寒暄。
- 知道电话里姓名、数字、时间要主动确认。
